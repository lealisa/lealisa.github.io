    About

    1 Palantir builds data fusion platforms for integrating, managing, and securing any kind of data, at massive scale.

    2 MoonArk is a mini-time capsule created by a multidisciplinary team of collaborators, set to arrive on the Moon in 2021.

    I am currently working at Palantir Technologies1, designing software and tools that empower some of world’s crucial organizations to ask and answer their most complex questions.

    Prior to Palantir, I was designing voice-driven applications for the visually impaired with Conversant Labs, and building an interactive web museum for MoonArk2. I earned my degree in Communication Design from Carnegie Mellon University3, where I discovered my love for interaction, experience, print, furniture and motion design.

    Born in Moscow to Russian-Vietnamese parents, and spending most of my teenage and adult years in the U.S., I’ve always been interested in how people bridge gaps between different languages, cultures and backgrounds. In college, I became fascinated with how design can not only drive positive change, but can also bring people of diverse identities and experiences together.
