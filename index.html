About

Previously, I worked at Palantir Technologies1 designing enterprise software for commercial, philanthropic, and government institutions. I earned my degree in Communication Design from Carnegie Mellon University2, where I discovered my love for creating interactive experiences, as well as front-end development, visual design, and motion. In my work, I enjoy being involved in every step of the design process—from strategy and ideation, all the way through prototyping and aligning the tiniest pixel in code.

Born in Moscow to Russian-Vietnamese parents, and immigrating to the U.S. in high school, I’ve always been interested in how people bridge gaps between different languages, cultures and backgrounds. In college, I became fascinated with how design can not only drive positive change, but can also bring people of diverse identities and experiences together.
